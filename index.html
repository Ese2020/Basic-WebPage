How to make Chin-Chin

Chin chin is a very popular snack in Nigeria, itâ€™s made from basic combination of flour, milk and sugar. There are other optional ingredients like egg, baking powder and nutmeg, these are strictly based on preference.
Typically, it can be either hard or crunchy. This recipe is a fine medium between the two. If you would prefer your final product to be much harder, omit baking power, do not use egg and add 1/2 cup more flour.

Ingredients:

  • 500g of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of Baking Powder
  • 100g of Sugar
  • 1 sachet of Milk Powder
  • 250g Butter
  • Little Condensed Milk
  • 1 or 2 Eggs

Preparations:

  1. Combine flour, baking powder and sugar in a bowl. Slice in margarine and mix until well incorporated (it should look like breadcrumbs)
  2. Crack in egg. mix
  3. gently add water into flour mix until you have a slightly stretchy but not sticky dough (do not add too much water at once)
  4. Roll out the dough on a flat surface, cut into desired shapes
  5. Set the oil on medium heat, wait until the oil is hot. Fry the shaped dough a batch at a time until golden brown
